Classic Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description:

A simple and flavorful Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes, and saut√© until the garlic is golden but not burnt.
4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet, tossing to coat with the garlic oil.
5. Season with salt and black pepper, then toss in the chopped parsley.
6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
