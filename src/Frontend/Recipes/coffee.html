Coffee-Classic Cappuccino

Description:

A rich and creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a layer of frothy foam. Perfect for a morning pick-me-up or an afternoon treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot (30 ml) espresso
  • 150 ml whole milk
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Cocoa powder or ground cinnamon (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Prepare 1 shot of espresso using an espresso machine or a strong brewed coffee.
2. Heat the milk until hot but not boiling, then froth it using a frother or by whisking vigorously.
3. Pour the hot espresso into a cup. Add the steamed milk, holding back the foam with a spoon.
4. Spoon the foam on top of the coffee.
5. Sprinkle with cocoa powder or ground cinnamon.
6. Enjoy your cappuccino hot.
