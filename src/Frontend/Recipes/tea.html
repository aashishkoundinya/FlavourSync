Masala Tea (Chai)

Description:

A warm and spiced Indian tea made with black tea, milk, and aromatic spices. Perfect for a comforting and invigorating drink.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tsp black tea leaves or 2 tea bags
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, crushed
  • 2-3 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 tsp sugar (or to taste)

Instructions:

1. In a pot, bring the water to a boil.
2. Add the ginger, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
3. Add the black tea leaves or tea bags and simmer for another 2 minutes.
4. Pour in the milk and bring the mixture to a boil.
5. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
6. Strain the tea into cups and serve hot.
