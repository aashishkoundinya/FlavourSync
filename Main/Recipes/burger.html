Classic cheese burger

Description:

A juicy and flavorful cheeseburger made with a beef patty, melted cheese, and fresh toppings. Perfect for a satisfying meal any time.

Ingredients:

  • 400 g ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
2. In a bowl, mix the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Form into 4 equal patties.
3. Brush each patty with olive oil and place them on the grill or skillet.
4. Cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 70°C (160°F).
5. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking, allowing it to melt.
6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
7. Assemble the burgers by placing each patty on a bun and adding lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles (optional).
8. Add ketchup and mustard if desired, then serve hot.
