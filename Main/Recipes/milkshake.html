Vanilla Milkshake

Description:

A creamy and refreshing vanilla milkshake made with rich vanilla ice cream and milk. Perfect for a sweet treat on a hot day.

Ingredients:

  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 200 ml whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Maraschino cherry (optional)

Instructions:

1. In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, whole milk, and vanilla extract.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
4. Top with whipped cream and a maraschino cherry if desired.
5. Serve immediately with a straw.
