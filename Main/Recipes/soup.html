Soup

Description:

A warm and comforting soup made with ripe tomatoes, fresh basil, and a hint of garlic. Perfect for a cozy meal or as an appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500 ml vegetable broth
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 100 ml heavy cream (optional)

Instructions:

1. Heat olive oil in a pot and saut√© chopped onions and garlic until soft.
2. Add chopped tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper; cook for 10 minutes.
3. Pour in vegetable broth; bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Blend the soup until smooth.
5. Stir in basil and cream (optional); simmer for 5 minutes.
6. Serve hot, garnished with extra basil if desired.
