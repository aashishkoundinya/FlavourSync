Pizza-Classic Margherita

Description:

A classic Margherita pizza with a crispy crust, topped with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil. Perfect for a simple yet delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
4. Arrange the mozzarella slices and tomato slices on top of the sauce.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and oregano (optional).
6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
7. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan (optional).
8. Slice and serve hot.
